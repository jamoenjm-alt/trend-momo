How the Signal System Works

Every cell is calculated from Moving Average (MA) crossovers — comparing a fast MA to a slow MA. When price or fast MA is above the slow MA, that signal is bullish (+1). Below = bearish (−1). The regime badge is the average of all relevant signals.

The columns run fastest to slowest: Canary (the board's "1–2 Week" column) → ST Trend ("1–3 Month") → LT Trend ("3–9 Month") → All Indicators ("All Signals").

Signals are lagging — they confirm a trend that has already started. The edge is in riding confirmed trends, not catching tops and bottoms.

Bull Entry Signals (Bear → Bull Reversal)

1
Canary turns Bull while ST and LT still Bear = first alert. Don't enter yet — this flips back often. It's a watch signal only.
2
ST Trend turns Bull while LT still Bear = building conviction. Canary + ST both bull = consider a small starter position.
3
LT Trend turns Bull = confirmed trend change. All columns STRONG BULL = trend fully confirmed — comfort for positions you already hold. As a fresh entry it's late: fully-extended names had the weakest forward returns in our backtests.
4
Stability dot turns light green = regime has held 10+ days. This is your size-up signal — trend is confirmed, not a false alarm.
⚡ Fastest reversals: all columns flip bull within days. These are momentum explosions. Enter early but size smaller until LT confirms.

Sell & Exit Signals (Bull → Bear Reversal)

1
Canary turns Bear while ST and LT still Bull = early warning. Tighten your stop, reduce 10–20%, watch closely.
2
ST Trend turns Bear = meaningful exit signal. Canary + ST both bear = reduce 30–50%. Short-term trend has turned against you.
3
LT Trend turns Bear = confirmed exit. This is the signal to exit the majority of your position.
4
All Indicators STRONG BEAR = you missed the ideal exit — still exit now. Don't hold hoping for a bounce.
🔴 Stability dot turns red while bearish = downtrend is well-established and reliable. This is not a dip.

Price Reversal Patterns

Momentum Surge (V-shape): Canary → ST → LT all flip bull within 1–2 weeks. Fast and powerful. High conviction entry once LT confirms. Often follows major news or earnings beats.

Slow Grind (Staircase): Each column flips over 2–4 weeks. More reliable. Stability dot progresses yellow → light green → dark green. Best risk/reward entries.

Bear Trap: Canary + ST flip bull, stability dot still red , then flip back bear within days. Failed reversal — never size up on a red dot.

Distribution Top: Canary goes bear, ST follows a week later, LT holds bull for 2–4 weeks. Classic staged distribution. Exit in stages: reduce at Canary, half-exit at ST, full exit at LT.

Reading Column Combinations

SBSBSB + Established uptrend, dark green dot. Strongest confirmation for existing holdings — but as a fresh entry it's late; extended names mean-reverted in our tests.

SBSBWBR + Strong short-term, LT still turning. Size smaller until LT confirms.

WBRWBRSB + Canary + ST have flipped bear but LT still bull. Key exit warning. Reduce now.

SWSWSW + All sideways + red dot = choppy. No edge. Do nothing.

SBRSBRSBR + Dark green dot on bear = sustained downtrend. No longs.

What to Be Cautious Of

🔴 Red stability dot = no trade. When the dot is red, the signal is chopping back and forth. Any entry is a coin flip.

All STRONG BULL = likely late. By the time every column is STRONG BULL with dark green dot, the easy move has already happened. Look for pullback entries, not chasing.

Signals lag. A stock can drop 20% and still show STRONG BULL for a week. Use stops, not signals alone, for risk management.

Crypto moves faster. Signals can flip in days not weeks. Higher false-signal rate. Use smaller positions and wider stops.

Market correlation. In a broad market crash (SPY STRONG BEAR), almost everything goes red regardless of individual signals. Check Indices first.

News events. Earnings or macro data can cause a 1-day spike flipping Canary. If Canary flips but ST/LT don't follow in 3–5 days, it's noise.

▶  📖 How to Use Indicators — Beginner’s Guide

What Is This Dashboard?

This board shows whether each asset is in a bullish or bearish regime using Moving Average crossover signals — the same method used by professional trend-following funds.

It doesn’t predict the future. It tells you what the trend is right now and how stable that has been over 30 days. Hover any signal cell to see a breakdown of each MA pair.

The Four Signal Columns

1–2 Week (Canary) — Earliest warning (3/10 & 5/15 day MAs). Turns first. Expect false alarms.

1–3 Month (ST Trend) — Short-term trend over weeks (42–63 day MAs). Strong signal a move is happening now.

3–9 Month (LT Trend) — Long-term trend over months (84–252 day MAs). Slowest to change, most reliable. Most important column.

All Signals — Average of every MA pair. Overall trend health score.

The Regime Badges

STRONG BULLAll signals bullish. Highest conviction uptrend.
WEAK BULLMostly bullish. Trend up, moderate conviction.
SIDEWAYSMixed signals. Choppy — wait for clarity.
WEAK BEARMostly bearish. Reduce long exposure.
STRONG BEARAll signals bearish. Exit longs.

The Stability Dot

Compares today’s regime to 10, 20, and 30 days ago.

Dark green — Unchanged 30+ days. Established trend. Best entries.
Light green — Unchanged 10–30 days. Trend holding.
Yellow — Regime changed within the last 10 days. Watch closely.
Red — Multiple flips in 30 days. Choppy — avoid trading.

Hover the Tooltip

Hover any coloured signal cell to see the Trend Ladder. Each row shows one MA pair:

• Pair (e.g. 10/3) = slow MA / fast MA periods in days

• Trend badge = green if price is above the slow MA (bullish), red if below

• Momo badge = green if fast MA is above slow MA (momentum building), red if below

• % vs slow = how far price is above/below the slow MA right now

Balance Sheet, P/E & News

Balance Sheet (0–10) — Financial quality. 8–10 = fortress. 1–4 = stress. N/A for ETFs, crypto, forex.

P/E — How expensive vs earnings. Context matters for growth stocks. Hover for detail.

News — Headline sentiment via Finnhub. Gut-check only — news lags price. Enter key in ⚙ Keys.

Quick Practical Rules

✅ All columns STRONG BULL + dark green dot + strong balance sheet = fully confirmed uptrend. Reassurance for holders — not an entry signal; fully-extended names had the weakest forward returns in our backtests.

⚠️ Canary goes bear while LT still bull = early warning. Don't sell yet — wait for ST to confirm.

🔄 Yellow/red dot + new bull signal = unconfirmed. Wait for green dot before sizing up.

🚫 All STRONG BEAR = exit. Red dot + STRONG BEAR = confirmed sustained downtrend, no longs.

🔁 Check Global Indices (SPY, QQQ) first. Individual signals are unreliable in a full market crash.
